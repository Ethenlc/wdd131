Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase my skills in programming and embedded systems by presenting detailed project pages, my resume, and a contact form for potential employers or collaborators to reach out to me. It will also serve as a central hub for my personal portfolio.

Scenarios

  • Visitor Question: What types of projects has Ethen completed?
    Answer: The site will provide a Projects page with sections for Programming and Embedded Systems, highlighting examples like the Minesweeper game, coin-counter, and paper airplane with live video streaming.
  • Visitor Question: How can I contact Ethen for a collaboration or job opportunity?
    Answer: A Contact page will include a form to send inquiries and links to my email and LinkedIn profile.
